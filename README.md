Students face challenges in obtaining scholarship due to intricate application process, and a
lack of awareness about diverse scholarship program further limits their access to educational
funding. The absence of a streamlined platform compounds the difficulties. Our comprehensive
app, designed with a user-friendly scholarship module, seeks to revolutionize educational
funding by simplifying scholarship application process and awareness. By seamlessly
connecting students, NGOs, and donors, our platform not only predicts scholarship approval
chances but also provides detailed reports, empowering students with information to make
informed choices about their educational funding opportunities.
